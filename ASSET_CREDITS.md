# Asset Credits

The starter fighters use 2D source art, then render as chunky sprite-voxel 3D fighters at runtime.

- `public/characters/kiro/animation-sheet.png`: User-provided local testing sprite sheet used for Kiro's frame animations and image-to-voxel extraction. Do not publish this asset publicly unless the project owner confirms they have rights to use and distribute it.
- `public/characters/kiro/frames/`: Locally extracted transparent frame PNGs derived from `animation-sheet.png` for idle, movement, attacks, hit, down, win, and lose preview states.
- `public/characters/riven/animation-sheet.png`: User-provided local testing sprite sheet used for Riven's frame animations and image-to-voxel extraction. Do not publish this asset publicly unless the project owner confirms they have rights to use and distribute it.
- `public/characters/riven/frames/`: Locally extracted transparent frame PNGs derived from `animation-sheet.png` for idle, movement, attacks, hit, down, win, and lose preview states.
- `public/characters/astra/sprite-sheet-online.png`: Previous Astra source, "Ninja [Animated]" by DezrasDragons, OpenGameArt, CC0. Source: https://opengameart.org/content/ninja-animated
- `public/characters/dax/sprite-sheet-online.png`: "Ninja [Animated]" by Morgan McGuire and DezrasDragons, OpenGameArt, CC0/public domain. Source: https://opengameart.org/content/ninja-animated-0
- `public/stages/dungeon-world/dungeon_warkarma.glb`: Dungeon Warkarma from the Three.js example model collection. Used as the real 3D world around the centered fight ring.
